About

I'm a self-directed developer based in Menasha, Wisconsin, building things at the intersection of electronic music, artificial intelligence, and decentralized technology.

My work spans from real-time audio synthesis—TB-303 emulation in Rust/WASM, Java Android synths—to AI-driven creative tools for generative music, computer vision, and image generation, to psychedelic visual engines with 39 shader presets and counting.

I'm a member of the Appleton Makerspace, the largest community-run non-profit DIY workshop in northeast Wisconsin. All my projects are open source.
